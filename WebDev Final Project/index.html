Welcome to Kim Il-Kwon's Taekwon-Do

Kim Il-Kwon's dojos provide its students with world-wide seminars,
championships, summer and winter training camps, and numerous other
national and international activities and events. Master Kim Il-Kwon,
president and founder of the organisation, has documented over sixty
instructional videos, authored thirteen best selling books, and other
educational material to enhance learning and broading the horizons of all
martial arts students. If learning Taekwon-Do at a dojo is not for
you then you can find many instructional videos here
alternatively you can hit the change button to change the link to a
YouTube channel that features a variety of martial arts if even
Taekwon-Do is not your style.
